Our Management Team

Jerry Jones

President

Jerry is the Founder and Managing Partner of Western Technologies Group LLC. He is a Certified GIS Specialist with more than 30 years of experience in the field.

Jerry has maintained a statewide collection of tax maps and tax assessment data for over 20 years, leveraging the information to build real property products (NJPropertyFax.com) and services (NJFlood.com). Creating and maintaining a seamless statewide taxmap for New Jersey since 2006 has raised the standard of products offered by Western Technologies by producing accurate geocoding for every property in the State.
